Despite the immense disrepair of the castle, this garden seems to be well manicured and cared for. Sweet scents from unknown species of flowers hang in the air and the night seems less stressful.

While walking around the garden, you notice four main garden beds:

  • A patch of Marigolds
  • A patch of Tulips, Lavender, and Daffodils
  • A patch of Azaleas
  • A patch of Roses
