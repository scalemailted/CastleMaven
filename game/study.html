This once great library has been reduced to decrepit shelving and moldy paper and the air is musty and heavy with dust, as if undisturbed for many years. Though a few of the better bound books have survived, most have been reduced to dust and decay.

While searching, you find a curious panel on the back of the ruined bust. It contains a 3x3 set of buttons that stick down when pressed. Also underneath the panel is a large button that doesn't seem to do anything. Maybe if I find the correct combination of buttons, something will happen.
